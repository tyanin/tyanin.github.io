About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate Computer Science Masters student with a deep commitment to making technology accessible for everyone. My research focuses on accessibility in computer science, and I'm particularly inspired by innovations like Microsoft's Xbox Adaptive Controller that break down barriers and create inclusive experiences.

Currently pursuing my Master's degree, I'm dedicated to understanding how we can design and develop technology that serves all users, regardless of their abilities. My dream is to join Microsoft's accessibility team, where I can contribute to groundbreaking projects that transform how people with disabilities interact with technology.

When I'm not studying or researching, you'll find me exploring new accessibility tools, contributing to open-source projects, and advocating for inclusive design practices in the tech community.

My Accessibility Commitment

This website itself serves as a demonstration of inclusive design principles. It features keyboard navigation, screen reader optimization, high contrast support, scalable fonts, and respects user preferences for reduced motion. Every element has been carefully crafted to ensure equal access for all visitors.
